POTTERY STUDIO №1 IN ALMATY

Where does our story begin?

Pottery is considered one of the oldest crafts in the world. It originated in the Neolithic era, which is confirmed by numerous finds made during archaeological excavations: hand-sculpted clay figurines, primitive cooking utensils, vessels for storing water and grain.

Our workshop opened in 2018. The founders of the workshop are a prosperous artist - Ayazhan, a person who always pursues creativity - Altyngul and a sculptor - Nazym. At first, we only sold our products. Then people started asking how we do it. And we began to arrange master classes that imperceptibly changed us, because classes with people are always useful for both sides of the process. We open and teach to charge and charge in return.

Pottery became popular in the networks and together with it we also rose. People called, were interested and signed up for master classes with us. Each time there were more people from different cities. We decided to open our branches in two more cities. In 2021, we opened in Astana and Taraz.
